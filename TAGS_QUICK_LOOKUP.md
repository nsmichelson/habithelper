## Quick Selection Flowchart

### Choosing Tips by Constraint

**User has < 5 min?**
- Any area is fine (all have 0-5min options)

**User wants something daily?**
- Exercise (daily or weekly)
- Relationships (daily)

**User is in chaos/crisis?**
- Look for chaos_proof feature
- All areas have at least some chaos_proof tips

**User wants minimal planning?**
- Look for no_planning feature
- All areas have this

**User situation is unsafe (abuse concern)?**
- Use ONLY relationship tips with safety contraindications noted
- Emphasize professional support
- Avoid advice that requires vulnerability

**User wants most detailed guidance?**
- Nutrition (most mechanisms, most involves tags)

**User wants quick wins?**
- Exercise (quick, accessible, varied)

**User needs flexibility?**
- Nutrition (most time options, most where options)
- Organization/Productivity (as_needed sustainability)
