## Key Insights

### 1. Nutrition is the Most Detailed Area
- 28 goals (vs 6-13 in other areas)
- 43 mechanisms (vs 11-30 in other areas)
- 75 involves tags (vs 0-41 in other areas)
- Most granular, implementation-focused

### 2. Relationships is the Most Safety-Conscious
- 10/10 tips have contraindications mentioning abuse/safety
- Only area emphasizing professional support for unsafe situations
- Smallest goal set (4) and mechanism set (11)
- Fastest to execute (0-5min, 5-15min only)

### 3. Exercise is Most Accessible
- 7 features (most) including family/kid-friendly options
- No complex requires
- Quick to do (0-5min, 5-15min)
- Most "involves" tags (41 activity variations)

### 4. Organization/Productivity are Similar
- Both focus on workspace/task management
- Both have minimal contraindications
- Both offer flexibility (as_needed, nightly)
- Both require minimal equipment (timer only)

### 5. Universal Design Principles
All areas emphasize:
- Quick wins (0-5min, 5-15min options)
- Chaos-proof design (all have this feature)
- No planning required (all have this)
- Solo-friendly (all have this)
- Free or low cost ($)
- Impulse-friendly (all have this)

This aligns with the app's mission to be approachable and unintimidating for all users.
