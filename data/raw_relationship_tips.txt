Tip 1 – Soft‑Start Your Next Complaint
summary: Trade blame for clarity with a gentle, need‑based opener today.
details_md:
The Experiment: Use a Gottman‑style softened startup: “I feel [emotion] about [situation]. I need [specific request] so that [shared benefit].”
Why it Works: Softened startups reduce defensiveness and escalation by leading with feelings, not accusations. They align with Gottman’s behaviorally specific scripts and IBCT’s acceptance‑plus‑change stance. Clear requests invite success; shared benefits maintain teamwork.
How to Try It:

Draft two versions; pick the calmer one.

Speak slowly and sit down.

End with “Does that make sense?”
